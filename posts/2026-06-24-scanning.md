Welcome to my new blog. This will take the place of daily updates so that readers have control over how frequent they would like to engage with my updates. 

I am starting to feel self conscious about knocking on everyone's email-front door to record fairly mundane updates now that Scanning and Labeling are the top two chores on our hitlist. 

Building out this little site also breaks the monotony of some of the more tedious work and allows me to explore how cloud based tech might aid my efforts and the efforts of other volunteers around the district.

Today, I scanned and ingested over 500 books in just over 1.5hrs of work. I had an especially high book data enrichment hit rate when scanning ISBNs and I'm getting used to scanning fast now that I have sounds to let me know when a book fails to resolve all necessary data from a scanned ISBN.
