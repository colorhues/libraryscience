## Here's the big goal

The big goal is to organize the library stacks so that books can be checked in, checked out, and general library usage tracked by student and classroom / grade level.

## What is needed to achieve this goal?

1. 🟡 **IN PROGRESS:** The physical stacks must be accurately represented in the digital catalog within Destiny.
2. 🟢 **DONE:** Destiny activities require functioning hardware, eg: a working scanner (or two).
3. 🟡 **IN PROGRESS:** The physical stacks need an organizational system that kids can easily learn and interpret.
